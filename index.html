Melons are our friends.

In 2014, the average human consumed 22.75 pounds of melons. That's a lot of melons. A little... too much. Thoughout history, mankind has been introduced to many foodstuffs. But one of the arguebly best advancements thus far in human history, has been the domestication of melons.

Our Mission

As the consumption of melons increases, we think it's time to put a stop to the cruelty and mistreatment of our 91%-water-and-6%-sugar friends.
